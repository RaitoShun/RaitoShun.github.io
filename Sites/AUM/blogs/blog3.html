What is AUM?

AUM — the eternal Universal mantra. It’s chanted during extended meditations because it’s very sound contains the entire universe. However, I believe there’s more to this sacred vibration that can profoundly change our lives.

The most enlightened yogis tell us AUM is made up of the three fundamental components that form ALL human language: the “ah” sound, the “oh” sound, and the “mm” sound. Even though our tongues can mix and alter these three sounds into millions of words, these three sounds alone are the most intrinsic parts. Gurus also teach us that AUM represents the three stages of the universe: Birth, Life, and Death.

The “ah” is the very first sound that babies make, regardless of their nationality. The “mm” sound represents the quantum vibrations of the universe after we pass away. And the “oh” sound is everything manifested in between. These truths are beautiful and enriching. Yogis and gurus have been teaching these esoteric mysteries for years, even though so much of the world has difficulty listening. But as I was meditating one day on the eternal mantra of AUM, I received a message from the universe that AUM meant something even more. “How can AUM help me and those I love today, right now?” I asked silently. I didn’t expect a response. However, as I sat there in the dark and in the silence, I received a message that AUM stood for a state of existence that our planet desperately needs right now: Awakened Ultimate Manifestation.

This state of existence comes in three parts:


Awakened


Each one of us has the obligation to step out of the social paradigms and the matrix that surrounds us. We need to wake up from the hypnosis that our current world order has placed us in.




Ultimate


Once awakened, we have the obligation to find who we are at the deepest core. We must peel back the layers and remove anything that comes from somewhere other than the eternal love of the Universe.




Manifestation


Once we are awakened, and once we have learned who we truly are, we have the obligation to shine our light and share our gifts with the world. It is our duty as human beings on this planet to embody the spiritual truths we have come to share.



I was stunned. It seemed so simple, yet so profound. Here, in the eternal word of AUM, which I have chanted for so many years was a new meaning directed specifically for this time and place in human history. Having received this message, I knew that it was my calling to share this truth with others and help people walk through this process. That’s why you’re reading these words right now. I want to help each person on this amazing, spiritual journey, wherever they are at. Using the gifts of the Spirit which God has given me–and which you can receive too–we can raise the collective consciousness of our planet and bring it back into balance through healing. There’s so much we can do together.
