3 Ways to Boost Your Manifestation

Are you trying to manifest something in your life and feel like nothing’s happening? Do you think you’re doing something wrong? Are you starting to believe that manifestation does not work after all?

The first thing you should know is that it’s alright to feel this way. Frustration is a normal human emotion. Try to let it come and go without letting it make you feel disillusioned. Trust the process even if you don’t have a lot of evidence for it yet. With that said, here are three ways that help you boost your manifestation:



1. Act as though you already have it


You may have heard that manifestation requires that you think you already have what you want. However, thinking alone will not help you get it. Only thinking about it shows that you don’t have enough conviction, both to yourself and the Universe. Take action to prove your conviction for manifesting what you desire. For example, you want to manifest your dream vacation to the Bora Bora island. If your approach is only to dream about the blue sea there in your free time, you might not be successful at manifesting the vacation. A good approach will involve taking action as if you’re just about to leave for the vacation. Go through different websites to learn more about the island. Search for the hotel you would book. Take out the clothes you would wear. Make an itinerary. Plan out everything you would do.



2. Work on Yourself


Oprah has famously said: “Luck is preparation meeting the moment of opportunity. There is no luck without you being prepared to handle that moment of opportunity.” If you want to manifest something you want, you have to be ready to receive it. You have to become the kind of person that has what you want in their life. Think about the type of person who would travel to the Bora Bora island. A person who would go on a vacation like that likes adventure and new experiences. They are curious about different cultures. They enjoy being out in nature. Now think if you are that kind of person. Do you seek adventure and new experiences? Do you spend time learning about new cultures or out in nature? If you only dream about doing these things one day, it will be hard to manifest them in your life. Work on yourself so that you will be able to receive what you want when the opportunity arises.



3. Eliminate doubt


The fastest way to kill your dreams is to doubt that they will come true. Doubt is also the sneaky reason that may be standing in your path towards manifesting what you want. Faith is the foundation for manifestation. If you don’t have faith, your efforts will not result in the outcome you want. It is not always easy to prevent doubts from creeping in. That’s why you have to be on your guard. Avoid listening to arguments or rationalizations against what you want to manifest. Have faith. Trust the process. The Universe will show up for you.
